About me

I am a Lecturer at Sorbonne University, affiliated with the Machine Learning – Deep Learning and Information Access (MLIA) team of the ISIR lab. My research focuses on machine learning, particularly in the areas of image understanding and multi-modal data integration, including text-image and multi-sensor inputs. I am particularly interested in developing hybrid models that combine both physical and statistical approaches. Additionally, I work on enhancing the robustness of machine learning models to ensure their reliability across diverse applications. Prior to joining Sorbonne University, I was an Assistant Professor at the CEDRIC laboratory of the Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers (CNAM), where I was part of the Vertigo Team.
